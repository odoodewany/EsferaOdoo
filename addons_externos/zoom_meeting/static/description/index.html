Set Zoom Meetings Directly from Odoo Calendars

The Internet has really shortened the distance! Online Meeting platforms have proved the stated idiom true as you can connect to anyone from anywhere in the world.
Zoom is one of the most used online meeting platforms for official interaction with teams or with clients. If you are managing your business in Odoo and schedule official meetings via Zoom then updating all the details of the meeting manually becomes very tedious.
Hence, integrating Zoom with Odoo to manage business functions with Zoom meetings in a centralized way. Odoo Zoom Meeting Integration Module connects your Zoom Account with the Odoo instance.
It allows you to create a Zoom meeting directly from Odoo Calendar; moreover, you can also manage the meeting settings directly from Odoo.

Manage the Zoom Meeting Operations from Your Odoo Instance

You don't need to toggle between Odoo and Zoom to manage the operations. The module offers the ease to manage the meeting operations directly from your Odoo.
You can manage meeting Agenda details, password, meeting duration, meeting recurrence, timing, time zone, topic, etc directly from Odoo. The email id of the participant is fetched from the partner field of Odoo.
Moreover, you can also manage the settings like alternative hosts, enable/disable video or audio, mute on entry, etc directly from Odoo.

Update or Delete Zoom Meeting Records Easily

The module offers you the flexibility to anytime update or delete the Zoom meeting records from your Odoo. You just need to click the dedicated button offered to perform the action.
The record saved for the meeting is synchronized well between both platforms.

Detailed Features List

Zoom Meetings From Odoo

  • The module allows you to create and manage Zoom Meetings directly from Odoo.
  • You can authenticate the integration for users as well as the company.

Set the Zoom Meeting Type

  • You can set the meeting type while creating a Zoom Meeting.
  • The meeting types are Instant Meeting, Scheduled Meeting, Time zone, Recurring Meeting with Fixed Time and without Fixed Time.

Zoom meeting Authentication With Odoo

  • You can authenticate Zoom Meetings with Odoo from the Users and Company Section.

Secure Your Meetings With Passwords

  • You can secure your Zoom Meetings with passwords.

Ease to Set Meeting Configuration Individually

  • The module allows you to set the configuration for each meeting individually.
  • You can configure options like Mute Upon entry, Host Video, Participant Video, and Watermark.

Update/Delete Zoom Meetings

  • You can update or even delete the zoom meetings directly from Odoo.
  • Moreover, you can perform the operations from Zoom as well as Odoo Platform.
